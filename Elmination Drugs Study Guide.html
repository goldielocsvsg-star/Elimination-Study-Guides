üö® Critical Drug Interactions

  • Loop Diuretics IV: Push SLOWLY (ototoxicity)

  • Zofran = Zone out the nausea
  • Reglan = Regular GI movement
  • Flagyl = NO alcohol = FLAGyl your alcohol away!
